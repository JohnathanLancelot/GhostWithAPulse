@ghost.with.a.pulse Online Art Portfolio

Hello, I'm Nathan Ki, a semi digital artist based in New Zealand. I illustrate a mix of humanoid and animal characters, and while I dabble in watercolour and fully digital art from time to time, I prefer to draw with pencils on paper, and then colour and shade my work digitally in Photoshop. I enjoy creating both spooky / horror-esque and innocent cutesie art.
